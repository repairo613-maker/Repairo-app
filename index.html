Quick Guide

1️⃣ Add Money

Go to Account > Deposit. Send money via UPI and submit the exact 12-digit UTR number.

2️⃣ Join Match

Go to Home. Select Mode & Entry Fee. Click on JOIN NOW and enter your exact Free Fire Name.

3️⃣ Get Room ID & Pass

Go to My Match page. The Room ID & Password will appear there exactly 10-15 minutes before the match starts.

4️⃣ Win & Withdraw

Play the match in Free Fire. If you win, admin will add prize money to your wallet. Go to Account > Withdraw to get money in your UPI.
